## Gotchas

- Sampler must compare cache against the **committed** DB value (fresh/autocommit connection), or you'll get false "stale" readings from your own uncommitted writes.
- Count DB hits app-side (atomic counter in `loader()`) — simplest accurate measure; don't rely on `pg_stat_statements`.
- Dark-mode charts: test BOTH themes with the site's `data-theme` toggle, not just the OS media query.
