This site is a personal blog. Here's what gets collected when you visit, and where it goes.

## Analytics

This site uses **Google Analytics** (Google LLC) to understand how visitors use the site — page views, reading progress on posts, and clicks on external/social links. Google Analytics uses cookies and may share data per [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Advertising

This site displays ads via **Google AdSense**, which may use cookies to personalize ads based on your visits to this and other sites. See [How Google uses information from sites that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Contact form

The contact form on the [/contact](/contact/) page submits your name, email, and message to **Google Apps Script**, which forwards it to the site owner's email. This data is not used for any other purpose. The form is protected by **Google reCAPTCHA v3**; see Google's [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms).

## Newsletter

The subscribe box embeds a **Substack** signup form. Substack handles and stores the email address you enter there directly — see [Substack's privacy policy](https://substack.com/privacy).

## Local storage

Your dark/light theme preference is saved in your browser's `localStorage`. This never leaves your device.

## Third-party fonts and icons

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts and icons from Font Awesome (cdnjs), which may log your IP address as part of serving those files.

## Contact

Questions about this policy: reach out via the [contact page](/contact/).
